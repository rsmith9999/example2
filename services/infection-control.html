Infection Control

At ElectroClean Glasgow, we understand the importance of maintaining a clean and healthy environment, particularly in light of recent events. That's why we offer a professional infection control service that's designed to help protect your home or business from harmful viruses and bacteria. Our team of experienced cleaners uses the latest equipment and techniques to ensure that your property is thoroughly cleaned and disinfected to the highest standards.

Our infection control service includes a thorough cleaning and disinfection of all areas of your property, paying particular attention to high-touch surfaces such as doorknobs, handles, and switches. We use non-toxic and eco-friendly cleaning products that are tough on germs and bacteria, but gentle on your property. With our service, you can rest assured that your property is being cleaned safely and effectively.

We understand that every property has different cleaning requirements, which is why we offer a flexible service that's tailored to your needs. Whether you require a one-off deep clean or regular cleaning services, we can work with you to create a cleaning plan that meets your specific requirements. We also offer competitive pricing and transparent quotes with no hidden costs, so you know exactly what you're paying for.

At ElectroClean Glasgow, we take pride in delivering a service that's both efficient and affordable. We understand the importance of maintaining a clean and healthy environment, and we're committed to helping our customers achieve this. Our team of experienced cleaners are fully trained and insured, so you can be confident that your property is in safe hands.

Finally, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all of our cleaning services. If you're not completely happy with the results, just let us know and we'll come back to make it right. With our infection control service, you can rest assured that your property is being thoroughly cleaned and disinfected, giving you peace of mind and helping to keep you and your family or employees safe.
