Tenancy Cleaning

Moving out of a rented property can be stressful, especially when it comes to cleaning. At ElectroClean Glasgow, we offer a comprehensive tenancy cleaning service that's designed to take the hassle out of end-of-tenancy cleaning. Our team of experienced cleaners will ensure that your property is cleaned to the highest standards, helping you to get your full deposit back and ensuring a smooth transition to your next home.

Our tenancy cleaning service includes a thorough cleaning of all rooms and surfaces, including floors, walls, carpets, and upholstery. We also clean kitchen appliances, bathrooms, and any other areas that require special attention. With our service, you can rest assured that your property will be cleaned from top to bottom, leaving it in immaculate condition for the next tenants.

We understand that every landlord or letting agent has different cleaning requirements, which is why we offer a flexible service that's tailored to your needs. Whether you require a basic clean or a deep clean, we can work with you to create a cleaning plan that meets your specific requirements. We can also provide a detailed cleaning checklist and photographic evidence of our work, to ensure that you're fully satisfied with the results.

At ElectroClean Glasgow, we take pride in delivering a service that's both efficient and affordable. We understand that moving house can be expensive, which is why we offer competitive pricing and transparent quotes with no hidden costs. We also use eco-friendly cleaning products and techniques wherever possible, in order to minimize our impact on the environment and keep your home healthy and safe.

Finally, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all of our cleaning services. If you're not completely happy with the results, just let us know and we'll come back to make it right. With our tenancy cleaning service, you can rest assured that your property will be cleaned to the highest standards, leaving you free to focus on your move and your next adventure.
