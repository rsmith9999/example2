What do we offer?

ElectroClean Glasgow offers a range of cleaning services to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our services include:

  • Commercial cleaning: We provide regular office cleaning, retail cleaning, and other commercial cleaning services to help businesses maintain a clean and professional environment.
  • Biohazard cleaning: We're certified to handle biohazardous materials, including blood, bodily fluids, and other potentially hazardous materials.
  • Trauma cleaning: We offer discreet and compassionate trauma cleaning services to help individuals and families recover from traumatic events.
  • Infection control: We use advanced cleaning technology and methods to help prevent the spread of infectious diseases in healthcare facilities, schools, and other settings.
  • Tenancy cleaning: We offer end-of-tenancy cleaning services to help landlords and tenants ensure that properties are clean and ready for new occupants.
  • Oven cleaning and kitchen deep cleaning: We provide comprehensive cleaning services for kitchens, including oven cleaning, deep cleaning of floors and surfaces, and more.
  • High-level cleaning: We have the equipment and expertise to clean hard-to-reach areas, such as ceilings, walls, and high windows.

Whatever your cleaning needs may be, ElectroClean Glasgow has the expertise and equipment to get the job done right. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you maintain a clean, healthy, and safe environment.
