Who are we?

ElectroClean is a leading cleaning company that offers a cutting-edge approach to disinfection and sanitation. Using electrostatic technology, ElectroClean is able to provide a thorough and effective clean to a wide range of surfaces. Their unique approach involves using an electrostatic sprayer to apply a mist of EPA-approved disinfectants to hard-to-reach areas, ensuring that even the most inaccessible nooks and crannies are disinfected. This innovative method makes ElectroClean an ideal choice for businesses, schools, hospitals, and other organizations that require a high level of cleanliness and sanitation.

At ElectroClean, they understand the importance of maintaining a clean and healthy environment. That's why their team of trained professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional service that exceeds their clients' expectations. With ElectroClean, you can rest assured that your space is not only clean, but also free of harmful bacteria and viruses. Their electrostatic technology ensures that every surface is covered, and their EPA-approved disinfectants are effective against a wide range of pathogens. Whether you're looking to keep your employees safe, your customers healthy, or your students protected, ElectroClean has the expertise and resources to meet your needs. So, if you're looking for a reliable and effective cleaning solution, look no further than ElectroClean.

Commercial Cleaning

ElectroClean's commercial cleaning services are designed to help businesses of all sizes maintain a clean and healthy environment for their employees and customers. This includes regular office cleaning, kitchen deep cleans, and high-level cleaning.

Tenancy Cleaning

Our tenancy cleaning service is designed to help landlords and property managers get their properties ready for new tenants. We clean everything from top to bottom, including kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas, ensuring that the property is clean and ready for its new occupants.

Oven Cleans

Our oven cleaning service is designed to thoroughly clean ovens of all types, removing built-up grease and grime and leaving the oven looking and smelling like new.

Infection Control

This service is designed to help prevent the spread of infectious diseases in healthcare facilities, schools, and other public spaces. ElectroClean uses specialized equipment and disinfectants to thoroughly clean and disinfect surfaces and equipment, reducing the risk of infection.

Pest Control

ElectroClean provides professional pest control services to help eliminate and prevent infestations of insects and rodents in commercial and residential settings.

Trauma Cleans

ElectroClean offers specialized cleaning services for trauma scenes, including crime scenes and accidents. Our team is trained to safely and effectively clean and disinfect areas where blood, bodily fluids, and other biohazard's are present.

Biohazard Cleans

Similar to trauma cleans, biohazard cleans are designed to clean and disinfect areas where biohazard's are present. This includes cleaning up after industrial accidents, chemical spills, and other hazardous situations.

High Level Cleaning

This service is designed to clean hard-to-reach areas, such as ceilings, light fixtures, and ductwork. ElectroClean uses specialized equipment and techniques to safely and effectively clean these areas, helping to improve indoor air quality and prevent the buildup of dirt and debris.

Kitchen Deep Cleans

ElectroClean's kitchen deep cleaning service is designed to thoroughly clean and sanitize all areas of commercial kitchens, including floors, walls, and equipment. This helps to prevent the spread of germs and bacteria and ensures a clean and safe food preparation environment.
